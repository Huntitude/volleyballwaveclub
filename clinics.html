How to Register for any Clinic:

We are now making ALL registrations via our new SportsEngine Registration page. The link will be provided below. Refunds for any cancellations only IF the cancellation is made BY 6 PM on the TUESDAY EVENING prior to the Thursday clinic for which the player has previously registered. We are implementing this new process due to multiple late cancellations and no shows, causing us to fall short financially. We certainly understand emergency situations, and if this happens to you, please communicate with us immediately when you are able. Late refunds MAY be made, depending on the situation. Remember that we have to pay for the gym rental as well as coaches fees, so when someone does not show up, that affects the entire situation for us as a club. Thank you for your understanding in the matter.
