18 and Under Division (See WAIVER Details Below for 17's Exception)

Players who were born on or after July 1, 2006 AND a high school student in the twelvth (12th) grade or below during some part of the current academic year are eligible to compete in 18 & under or Players who were born on or after July 1, 2007, (who are defined as 18 & under by the USAV Age Definition) and are in the 11th grade for the current academic year are waivered to compete in 17 & under. (This age waiver is based on recruiting concerns for 11th grade girls previously required to participate in girls 18's qualifiers and the 18's GJNC.)
