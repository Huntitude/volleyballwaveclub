How to Register for USAV and Tryouts

Select Steps 1 & 2 to expand more information.

  1. Step 1

    Sign-up or Renew Player with USAV. (Make sure you note the six-digit USAV Number for Tryout Pre-Registration.)
    Register or Renew Player Account with USAV, found on OKRVA website. Be sure to PRINT a copy of the forms. There is a USAV annual membership fee paid directly to USAV when registering. The registration/renewal asks for the player's club team. Leave that blank until player has committed to a specific team/club. Make note of the player's USAV Membership Number, as we will need that as evidence of being registered for the upcoming season.
    IMPORTANT: A player will NOT be allowed to participate in tryouts without having a current 2022-2023 USAV Registration for the upcoming season.

  2. Step 2

    Determine Player's "Team Age".
    This is NEW Age Definition for players for the 2022-2023 season, based on the new July 1st date.
    We have an online tool that will show you the YOUNGEST age team on which your daughter can participate without an Exception Waiver granted by OKRVA Board.
    View the age calculator down below.

  3. Step 3

    Fill out Wave Tryout Pre-Registration Form.

    Please complete the Tryout Pre-Registration form.
    Tryout Dates have been approved. The dates are shown below.
    CLICK Here to Complete the Tryout Pre-Registration Form

  4. Step 4

    Come to the Tryout and Complete Registration at Table.
    IF you are UNABLE to attend a specific tryout, please notify Dave to see about coming to an alternate tryout event. (Email: director@wavevolleyball.net)

What does it cost to play with Wave?

Tryout Cost

The tryout cost per player is $25. Cash or check will be accepted on the day of tryouts. Checks must be made out to "Wave Volleyball Club".

Per Athlete Cost

One of our primary desire is to keep the cost as low as possible so that families can afford to be involved with Club Volleyball!
The cost per player for 2022-2023 will be $1,140! Practices will be primarily at Titan Sports Complex this coming season!

Here is what's included with this fee:

  • 2 Long Sleeve Jerseys
  • 1 Pair of Spandex
  • 1 Backpack
  • 1 Warm Up Top
  • 1 Practice Tee
  • Required Raffle Ticket Participation Minimum of $40
  • Regular Season Coaching Fees
  • Gym Rental for Two 2-hour Practices Per Week
  • Between 6 and 8 Tournaments plus Oklahoma Regionals in May

What is NOT Included:

  • Warmup Bottoms Uniform Upgrade.
  • Player and Family Travel Costs to and from Tournaments.
  • Coaches Travel Costs to and from Tournaments, including meals while away.
